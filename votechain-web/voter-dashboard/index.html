Voter Instructions: Enter your Voter ID and PIN underneath your desired Candidate, then press 'Submit'.

Once your Vote has been processed, a receipt will be printed containing your personal Ballot ID.

When polling closes, a bulletin of every ballot will be published on your consituencies web page. Here you can confirm the details of your Vote.
